About Rigmor

Rigmor Hotel

Our History

Established in the heart of Udaipur, Hotel Rigmor has been a symbol of warm hospitality and exquisite comfort since its inception.

Hotel Rigmor was built with a deep understanding of the rich cultural heritage of Udaipur, famously known as the "City of Lakes." Inspired by the city's historic charm and traditional architecture, we have blended classic design with contemporary facilities, ensuring that every guest enjoys both comfort and elegance.

Over the years, Hotel Rigmor has become a preferred choice for travelers, families, and business professionals seeking a serene stay near the bustling city center.

Local Amenities

At Hotel Rigmor, Udaipur, we ensure that our guests have access to a range of local amenities that enhance their stay. From modern conveniences to cultural attractions, everything you need is just a stone's throw away from our hotel.

  • Local Restaurants

    Udaipur is known for its rich culinary culture, and staying at Hotel Rigmor puts you in close proximity to some of the best local and multi-cuisine restaurants.

  • Nature

    Udaipur is renowned for its natural beauty, and Hotel Rigmor offers easy access to some of the city's most scenic spots. Just a short walk from the hotel is Gulab Bagh (Rose Garden), a lush green space with a mini-zoo perfect for a peaceful stroll.

  • Art and Culture

    Udaipur's rich history and vibrant culture surround Hotel Rigmor, offering guests easy access to iconic attractions. The City Palace Museum showcases royal artifacts and exquisite paintings, while Bagore Ki Haveli offers captivating Rajasthani dance, music, and puppet shows. For a glimpse of traditional art and craftsmanship.

Cancellation

At Hotel Rigmor, we understand that travel plans can sometimes change, and we aim to make the cancellation process as convenient as possible for our guests. We offer free cancellation up to 24 hours before the check-in date, ensuring you have the flexibility you need. In case of a late cancellation or no-show, a charge equivalent to one night's stay will apply. Any refunds, if applicable, will be processed within 7-10 business days to the original payment method..

Additionally, if you wish to modify your booking, such as changing your check-in date or room type, we are happy to accommodate you based on availability without any extra charges. Our goal is to provide a hassle-free and comfortable experience, allowing you to plan your trip with confidence.

Payments

At Hotel Rigmor, we offer a safe and hassle-free payment process for our guests. We accept credit cards, debit cards, net banking, UPI, mobile wallets, and cash for easy transactions. Any advance payment made during booking is securely processed, and refunds, if applicable, are completed within 7-10 business days. We ensure complete transparency in all payment processes, allowing you to book with confidence.

Check In / Out Rules

At Hotel Rigmor, our standard check-in time is 12:00 PM, and check-out time is 11:00 AM. Early check-in or late check-out is subject to availability and may incur additional charges. Guests are required to present a valid ID proof at the time of check-in. We strive to make your arrival and departure smooth and convenient, ensuring a pleasant stay throughout.

Disable Access

At Hotel Rigmor, we are committed to ensuring comfort and accessibility for all our guests. Our hotel offers disabled-friendly access, including wheelchair ramps, spacious elevators, and accessible washrooms for guests with limited mobility. Our staff is always available to assist and ensure a comfortable and convenient stay for everyone.
