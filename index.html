About us

Tailored Hospitality and an Unforgettable Stay

Nestled in the heart of Udaipur, Hotel Rigmore is a luxurious retreat that blends heritage charm with modern elegance.

Whether you are here for a relaxing getaway, a romantic escape, or a business trip, we ensure an unparalleled hospitality experience with world-class amenities and personalized services. From breathtaking views of Udaipur’s serene landscapes to indulgent dining and curated experiences, every moment at Rigmore is designed to be extraordinary. Our well-appointed rooms, warm hospitality, and attention to detail make us the perfect choice for discerning travelers..
